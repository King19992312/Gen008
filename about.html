About Us

This is what we do.

As students of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT), we are not just learners confined to the four walls of a classroom. We are advocates, innovators, and change-makers committed to making a tangible difference in the world around us. And one of the most pressing issues we have chosen to tackle head-on is the pervasive problem of plastic waste.

In our pursuit of knowledge and excellence in the field of IT, we recognize the interconnectedness of technology and sustainability. We understand that the solutions to today's environmental challenges require not only scientific expertise but also creativity, collaboration, and advocacy. That's why we have taken it upon ourselves to raise awareness about the detrimental effects of plastic waste and to champion initiatives aimed at reducing its impact on our planet.

Moreover, as future IT professionals, we recognize the power of technology to drive positive change. We explore innovative solutions such as mobile apps for waste management, AI-powered recycling systems, and blockchain-based supply chain tracking to address the complexities of plastic waste management. By harnessing the potential of technology, we aim to create scalable and sustainable solutions that can make a lasting impact.

But our advocacy goes beyond just raising awareness and implementing solutions. It is rooted in a deep sense of responsibility and stewardship towards our planet and future generations. We believe that by taking action today, we can create a brighter and more sustainable tomorrow for all.

As BSIT students, we are proud to be at the forefront of the movement for environmental conservation and plastic waste reduction. Together, we can inspire change, drive innovation, and lead the way towards a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable future for all. Join us in our mission to turn the tide on plastic waste and pave the way for a brighter tomorrow.
